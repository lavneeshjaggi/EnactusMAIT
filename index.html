About Us

Enactus MAIT

Enactus MAIT Delhi is the student chapter of Enactus, an international organization of student, academic and business leaders committed to using the power of entrepreneurial action to transform lives and shape a better and more sustainable world. We are a team of 40+ students working on social initiatives to impact the lives of people by helping them set up their own businesses and transforming them into successful entrepreneurs.

Current Ventures

    Alo

    A student during his education never actually learns what happens in a real life career and is never made aware of the skills and qualities required to succeed in life. This hinders a child's holistic development and forces the child to take various life affecting decisions based on his subject preferences and not career affinity. Alo brings to a child different hands-on activity based experiences of various professional fields which designed with respective experts aim to provide an authentic exposure of the career and development of real life skills specific to field. We also provide the student with a detailed assessment mapping his skill growth and career affinity.

    Arth

    Arth is a social enterprise run by students of IIT Delhi working to reduce deforestation caused by the fuelwood being used in cremations. Arth has devised a solution that goes with rituals of traditional hindu funerals. We aim to replace the fuelwood with logs made of cow dung, these logs have been modified for lower emissions. After a phase of in-depth analysis of the process of log production, we have worked on various parts. The most important among them being drying of the wet logs, major reason for not letting expansion of the idea throughout the country. Arth has come up with a drying solution that reduce the drying duration and hence not only fastens the entire process but also increases its ease.

    Nirmalaya

    This project is aimed at solving the problem of waste management in Delhi-NCR by setting up self-sustainable waste management entities through the action of decentralized waste segregation. We conduct awareness campaigns and educations session to provide knowledge to people about segregation, then we sell them our own designed composting solutions so that all the horticulture waste gets treated at home. These solutions are manufactured by small-scale manufacturers and potterers, hence making them our entrepreneurs.
