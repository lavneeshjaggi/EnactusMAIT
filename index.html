About Us

Enactus MAIT is the student chapter of Enactus, an international organization of student, academic and business leaders committed to using the power of entrepreneurial action to transform lives and shape a better and more sustainable world. We are a team of 30+ students working on social initiatives to impact the lives of people by helping them set up their own businesses and transforming them into successful entrepreneurs.
